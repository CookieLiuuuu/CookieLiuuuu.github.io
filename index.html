Research & Project Experience

Multimodal Image Retrieval with Point Cloud Assistance

Undergraduate thesis in autonomous driving and multimodal learning (Dec 2024 - Jun 2025). Designed a lightweight cross-modal attention fusion network with ResNet and PointNetVLAD for explicit image-point cloud alignment. Achieved Precision@5 > 95% on nuScenes and received the Outstanding Undergraduate Thesis award.

Sample-level Tail Encouragement for Long-tailed SSL (TASE)

Research project on long-tailed self-supervised learning (Sep 2024 - Oct 2025). Implemented sample-level adaptive temperature with pseudo-label guidance to improve tail-class representation learning. Validated on CIFAR10-LT, CIFAR100-LT, and ImageNet100-LT with results outperforming baseline methods.

Deep Learning and Robot Vision Applications (NUS)

Exchange research project at the National University of Singapore (May 2024 - Aug 2024). Configured and fine-tuned YOLOv7 for real-time robotic visual recognition, integrated MediaPipe for tracking and grasping, and optimized multi-model inference for deployment. Final grade: A+.
